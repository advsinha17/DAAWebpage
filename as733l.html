Dataset Description

The Autonomous systems AS-733 (Large) dataset consists of BGP logs of a communication network of AS exchanging traffic flows with its neighbours. The nodes are individual ASes (Autonomous System), and the edge between node i to j signifies that ith AS communicates with the jth AS. This dataset exhibits addition and deletion of nodes and edges over time. This dataset consists of 6474 nodes and 13895 edges. The details of the dataset are given in the table below.
